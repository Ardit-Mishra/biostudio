Visual & Design Specification for Ardit BioStudio
AI-Powered Molecular Intelligence Platform

Overall Identity:
• Ardit BioStudio represents my personal brand in AI-driven drug discovery: clean, modern, scientific, and uniquely mine.
• The interface blends bioinformatics precision with a calm, intelligent visual style.

Color Palette (Professional + Unique):
• Deep Navy (#1B2432) – primary headers, navigation, and structural elements.
• Slate Grey (#2E3A45) – panel backgrounds and container blocks.
• Soft Teal (#4FD1C5) – interactive accents, active states, highlights.
• Cool Blue (#4A90E2) – molecule outlines, ADME charts, feature visualizations.
• Off-White (#F5F7FA) – central workspace background for readability and clarity.
• Warm Gold (#F4C95D) – subtle highlights for important molecular insights.

Typography:
• Headers: “Inter” or “Montserrat” (bold, modern, scientific).
• Body Text: “Roboto” (clean, readable).
• Technical Inputs (SMILES, metrics): “JetBrains Mono”.

Platform Layout (Signature Ardit BioStudio Structure):
• Left Sidebar: Navigation labels styled under the BioStudio identity:
    – Molecule Studio
    – ADME Navigator
    – Toxicity Radar
    – Drug-Likeness Deck
    – Lead Lab
    – Explainability Canvas
    – Knowledge Graph
• Center Workspace: Molecule viewer and core property predictions.
• Right Insight Panel: Real-time summary card displaying formula, molecular weight,
  LogP, QED, toxicity tags, confidence score, and risk level.
• Footer: 
    “Created by Ardit • Ardit BioStudio • AI Molecular Intelligence • v1.0”

Component Styling:
• Rounded corners (8–12px) for clean, modern scientific visuals.
• Soft shadows for depth without clutter.
• Gradients in section headers: deep navy → slate grey.
• Teal or ultra-light-grey separators for organization.

Molecule Rendering:
• White RDKit canvas (#FFFFFF) with light border (#D9E0E7).
• Soft shadow behind the structure.
• Centered with optional 3D toggle.
• Gold atom/bond highlight on hover for premium feel.

Risk Indicators (Ardit BioStudio Signature Labels):
• 🟢 Safe Zone — Low Risk
• 🟡 Caution Zone — Medium Risk
• 🔴 Critical Zone — High Risk
Displayed as pill-shaped tags with gradient transitions.

Data Visualization Style:
• Thermometer-style ADME bars: blue → teal → yellow → red.
• UMAP/t-SNE labeled as “Molecule Constellation Map”.
• SHAP plots with teal + orange contrast.
• Smooth animations for hover and highlight states.

Interactive Features:
• “Interpret My Molecule” button: Generates a clear, natural-language analysis.
• Hover tooltips with simple explanations for scientific terms.
• Smooth fade transitions for panels (no distracting animations).
• Toggle: “Protein Intelligence Mode (ESM2) — Coming Soon”.

Knowledge Graph Panel:
• Visualizes compound → target → pathway using thoughtful BioStudio color coding.
• Teal nodes for compounds, blue for targets, gold for pathways.
• Curved edges for clarity and aesthetic flow.

Backend & Architecture:
• Optional FastAPI backend for modular enterprise-style deployment.
• Architecture diagram:
  SMILES Input → RDKit Features → ML Models (RF/XGBoost/NN) → Explainability → Lead Ranking → BioStudio Insights Panel.

Branding:
• Platform Name: **Ardit BioStudio**
• Subtitle: *AI-Powered Molecular Intelligence Platform*
• Gold underline accent beneath title for visual signature.

Goal:
Deliver a polished, scientific, modern, and uniquely ‘Ardit-made’ molecular intelligence platform. 
Ardit BioStudio is designed to showcase clarity, precision, creativity, and my vision for AI-driven drug discovery—distinctive and personal while aligned with real scientific workflows.
